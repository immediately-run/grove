When production breaks, follow this. It assumes you have read the [security policy](/content/handbook/security.mdx) and can reach the [engineering](/content/teams/engineering.mdx) on-call.

<Callout variant="warning" title="Declare early">A false alarm costs minutes. A late declaration costs the customer. When in doubt, declare.</Callout>

## The four steps

- **Declare** — open an incident channel, page the lead
- **Mitigate** — stop the bleeding before you find the cause
- **Communicate** — status page every `15 min`
- **Review** — a blameless write-up within `48 h`

## Roles

| Role | Who | Reach |
|---|---|---|
| Commander | on-call lead | PagerDuty |
| Comms | duty manager | status page |
| Scribe | any responder | incident channel |
