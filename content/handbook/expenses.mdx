What Meridian reimburses, the limits, and the approval path. New hires reach this from [onboarding](/content/handbook/onboarding.mdx).

## Limits

| Category | Limit | Approval |
|---|---|---|
| Meals (travel) | $75 / day | auto |
| Lodging | $240 / night | manager |
| Software | $500 / yr | manager |
| Equipment | — | finance |

## How to file

- Submit within `30 days` of the expense
- Attach a receipt for anything over `$25`
- Tag the project so finance can allocate it

<Callout variant="warning" title="Out-of-policy spend">Anything above the limits needs finance sign-off in advance. Filing after the fact risks a rejection.</Callout>
