Welcome to Meridian. This entry is the canonical first-week checklist. It links out to the policies and people you will need — follow the [expense policy](/content/handbook/expenses.mdx) before your first trip, and find your team in the [phone book](/content/directory.mdx).

<Callout variant="tip" title="Start here">Your manager will have tagged the entries relevant to your team. Open the sidebar and look under your team's namespace.</Callout>

## Day one

Collect your laptop from IT, sign the [security policy](/content/handbook/security.mdx), and set up your accounts. If a link looks dim and dotted — like [travel-policy](/content/travel-policy.mdx) — that entry doesn't exist yet; ask Grove to create it.

- Pick up hardware and badge
- Read and acknowledge [the security policy](/content/handbook/security.mdx)
- Add yourself to the [directory](/content/directory.mdx) with your `role` and `phone`

## Week one

Shadow an [engineering](/content/teams/engineering.mdx) standup, file your first expense, and read the [incident-response runbook](/content/processes/incident-response.mdx). The runbook matters even if you are not on-call.

### Filing your first expense

Expenses are submitted through the portal. The categories and limits live in [the expense policy](/content/handbook/expenses.mdx); a quick reference:

| Category | Limit | Approval |
|---|---|---|
| Meals (travel) | $75 / day | auto |
| Lodging | $240 / night | manager |
| Software | $500 / yr | manager |
| Equipment | — | finance |
